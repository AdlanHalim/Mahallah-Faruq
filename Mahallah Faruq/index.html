Mahallah Faruq

It is named after Umar Bin Al-Khattab Al-Faruq, the second Caliph of Islam, and is known for its strong Islamic environment and focus on academic excellence.
Here, we provide a large room, a strategic location near IIUM Center, and vehicle parking lot.

Room

Mahallah Faruq's standard room offering for four residents provides a basic yet functional living space for students. While the initial description mentions a bed, study table and chair, and a locker. It provides a comfortable and functional living space for students.

Strategic Location

Mahallah Faruq location enables residents to easily access crucial university facilities within walking distance allowing students to minimizes time spent commuting, allowing them to dedicate more time to studies, extra-curricular activities or personal well-being.

Parking Lot

Mahallah Faruq provides on-site parking lot on first-come, first-served basis. Some parking lot are prioritized for residents with specific needs, such as those with disabilities.

Mahallah Representative Committee

No need to go it alone! Mahallah Faruq has your back with its awesome Representative Committee, your elected peers who champion student concerns and organize fun events, making your college life vibrant and fulfilling.

Advocating for Student Needs

The committee brings together student concerns and suggestions, advocating for improvements in accommodation, facilities, food quality, and overall student experience. They effectively communicate residents' needs to the administration, ensuring their voices are heard and addressed.

Organizing Events

By organizing leisure activities and social gatherings, the committee helps residents unwind, de-stress, and build lasting friendships, contributing to their overall well-being and creating a vibrant atmosphere within Mahallah Faruq.

Facilitating Communication

The committee acts as a communication hub, promoting open dialogue and understanding between residents, the administration, and student organizations. They regularly update residents on important announcements and address concerns through various channels.
